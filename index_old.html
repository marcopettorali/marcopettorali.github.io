Marco Pettorali

Post-doc researcher

Department of Information Engineering, University of Pisa

Marco Pettorali received the master’s degree (cum laude) in computer engineering from the University of Pisa, in 2021.
He is currently working toward the PhD degree with the Department of Information Engineering, University of Pisa.
He has served as publicity chair for the 26th IEEE International Symposium on a World of Wireless, Mobile and Multimedia Networks (WoWMoM) and the 7th Workshop on Smart Service Systems (SmartSys) co-located with IEEE SMARTCOMP 2022.
